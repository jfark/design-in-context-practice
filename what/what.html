In dreams, a room is often interpreted as a representation of the dreamer's mind. Similarly, we talk about the Internet as if it were a kind of mind. We certainly spend enough time in it for it to be a room... I find many spaces here, however, remain flat and cold.

My practice pushes the boundaries of "flat" spaces such as websites and digital content, book & record covers, exhibition catalogues, textiles, and printed materials.

In the next 5 years, my design practice will have produced a body of work that is, while seemingly disparate in medium and focus, connected by the common thread of creating a space for feeling free to riff and dream, to encourage the joy in free association, and the subconscious meaning that can be ascertained as a result.

I would like to create a comfortable, jovial environment for my clients to feel free dream up their own "rooms."
